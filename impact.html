Educational Sponsorship

One of Our Beneficiaries

When we first met one of our beneficiaries, she was at risk of dropping out of secondary school. Her family had fallen on hard times, and school fees felt impossibly out of reach. Pick-One stepped in — covering her fees, providing her with the materials she needed, and pairing her with a mentor who checked in regularly.

She went on to sit her final examinations and passed with distinction. She is currently completing a vocational course in fashion design and has spoken about one day employing others from her community.

"I didn't think anyone would invest in me. Pick-One changed that thinking entirely. Now I invest in myself every day."
👨‍🔧
Vocational Training & Empowerment

Another of Our Beneficiaries

One of our vocational trainees came to us with no formal qualifications and no clear path forward. Within six months of joining our welding and electrical programme, he had completed his certification and secured his first contract with a local construction firm.

Today, he runs a small workshop employing two assistants — both of whom he recommended for our next training cohort. This is what sustainable, community-internal growth looks like.

"I didn't need charity. I needed a chance. That's exactly what Pick-One gave me."

Looking Ahead

Our Goals & Objectives

Where we are going — and what we're committed to achieving.

✅

Sponsor at least 10 additional students into secondary and tertiary education annually, with dedicated mentorship and progress tracking throughout.

✅

Train and certify 20 additional individuals in income-generating vocational skills, with post-training mentorship and livelihood support to ensure successful transitions to self-employment or formal employment.

✅

Dig and maintain water wells in at least 5 new rural communities, expanding clean water access to underserved populations who currently have no reliable source.

✅

Build a sustainable community-led maintenance network for all existing wells, ensuring water access remains uninterrupted long after Pick-One's direct involvement concludes.

✅

Establish a formal alumni network of beneficiaries who actively mentor the next cohort — creating a self-sustaining cycle of community upliftment.

✅

Diversify funding sources through partnerships with our partners, government grants, and diaspora giving, reducing reliance on any single donor stream.
