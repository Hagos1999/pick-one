📚 Pillar 1

Educational Sponsorship

Education is the most powerful equaliser we know. Pick-One identifies children and young adults from low-income households who would otherwise be denied access to quality schooling due to financial constraints — and we remove that barrier entirely.

Our sponsorships cover school fees, uniforms, learning materials, and — where needed — exam fees for secondary and tertiary-level education. We pair each sponsored individual with a mentor, and we track their progress to ensure the support translates into real academic achievement.

We believe education is not just about acquiring qualifications; it is about opening a young person's mind to possibility and giving them the tools to write their own story.

🎯 Sponsor at least 10 additional students into secondary and tertiary education annually, with dedicated mentorship and progress tracking.

🛠️ Pillar 2

Vocational Training and Empowerment

Not every path to a better life runs through a university. For many young people in underserved communities, a practical skill — mastered and marketable — is the fastest route to economic independence and dignity.

Pick-One's vocational training programme equips participants with hands-on skills in trades including tailoring, welding, electrical work, catering, and more. We cover the cost of training, tools, and certification where applicable. We also provide support in accessing start-up capital and business mentorship to help graduates turn their skills into sustainable livelihoods.

We work with vetted training centres and skilled craftspeople to ensure instruction meets real market standards — not just theory, but the practical ability to earn.

🎯 Train & certify 20 additional individuals in income-generating vocational skills, with post-training mentorship and livelihood support.

💧 Pillar 3

Clean Water for Rural Communities

Beyond education and skills, Pick-One recognises that basic infrastructure is a prerequisite for meaningful development. We have funded and overseen the digging of 25 water wells in extremely rural, underserved communities — providing clean, reliable water access to thousands of people who previously had none.

Each well is dug in partnership with the local community and built to last. Community members are trained in basic maintenance procedures, and we conduct follow-up visits to ensure the infrastructure remains functional and accessible to all residents.

Access to clean water reduces illness, frees up time (especially for women and children who traditionally travel long distances to collect water), and enables communities to redirect energy toward education, work, and growth.

🎯 Dig and maintain water wells in at least 5 new rural communities, expanding clean water access to underserved populations.
